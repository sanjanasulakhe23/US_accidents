US Accidents Data Analysis

Power BI Dashboard | 2016–2023

This dashboard presents an in-depth analysis of over 5 million US accident records to identify trends, high-risk areas, and weather conditions impacting severity.

🔍 Key Insights

  • 🗺️ Top affected states and counties
  • 📊 Severity trends over time
  • 🌧️ Impact of weather conditions
  • 🔘 Interactive slicers for custom views
